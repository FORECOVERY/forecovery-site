From Addiction to Redemption: My Journey Through FORE Uganda

David Okoye

Recovery Advocate

Three years ago, I hit rock bottom. Addiction had taken everything from me - my family, my career, and nearly my life. This is the story of how FORE Uganda helped me reclaim my future.

The Descent Into Addiction

My substance abuse began innocently enough - a drink to relax after work, painkillers for an injury. But what started as occasional use spiraled into a daily necessity. By 2020, I was consuming a bottle of whiskey and multiple pills just to function.

"Addiction isn't about substances - it's about the void we're trying to fill. FORE helped me understand that emptiness and how to heal it."

The Turning Point

My wake-up call came when I woke up in a hospital bed after overdosing. The doctors told me my liver was failing. That's when my sister introduced me to FORE Uganda's Tranquil Home program.

The Recovery Process

At FORE, I discovered a comprehensive approach to recovery:

  • Medical Detox: Safely managing withdrawal symptoms
  • Individual Therapy: Unpacking trauma and triggers
  • Group Sessions: Building community and accountability
  • Life Skills Training: Preparing for sober living

Life After Recovery

Today, I'm two years sober, reunited with my family, and working as a peer counselor at FORE. The organization didn't just help me stop using - they helped me rebuild my identity beyond addiction.

If you or someone you love is struggling with addiction, know that help is available. Visit our Programs page to learn about our services.
