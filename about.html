Empowering Recovery,

Restoring Lives.

Focus on Recovery (FORE) Uganda is a Non-Profit organisation on a mission

to tackle mental health and addiction crisis in Uganda. Founded in 2015 by

a group of individuals who personally experienced the challenges of

addiction and are now in recovery, FORE aims to tackle the growing

addiction and mental health crisis in Uganda.

Vision

A future where recovery is fully supported and celebrated, with accessible care for all, transforming lives and communities affected by addiction in Uganda

Mission

Empower individuals on their recovery journey, by providing compassionate, accessible and affordable care, helping them overcome addiction and rebuild their lives.

Strategic Objectives

1.To provide sustainable and impactful recovery services in Uganda, empowering individuals to overcome addiction through compassionate, affordable and culturally appropriate care, and enabling them to lead healthy, productive lives.

2.To dismantle the stigma surrounding addiction and mental health and promote open and honest discussions, empowering individuals to seek help and break the chains of addiction.

3.To advocate for and Influence Policy for integrated mental health and addiction recovery

Our Approach

Treatment

Treatment Through our Tranquil Homes inpatient department, we offer comprehensive care tailored to diverse needs, including individuals struggling with drugs and substance, trauma-informed treatment, support for gender-based violence survivors, neonatal depression management and interventions for suicidal ideation. ,

Our qualified and compassionate team, combined with a robust follow-up program, ensures holistic healing as we walk alongside our clients every step of the way. With a steadfast commitment to affordability, we make recovery accessible to all who seek it, ensuring that no one is left behind.
Read More about Our Approach..

Awareness and Advocacy

We work to amplify the voices of individuals and communities affected by addiction and mental health challenges, ensuring their needs and rights are at the forefront of local, national and global policies.

Strategic Partnerships

We collaborate with individuals, organisations, private companies and government agencies to expand access to mental health services. We provide employee wellness programs, mental health workshops, counselling services, peer support groups and subsidised or free mental health services tailored to the needs of underserved communities.
