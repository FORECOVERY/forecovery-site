Our Story

In 2015, a group of courageous individuals in Uganda, each bearing

the scars of addiction, came together with a shared vision: to turn
their pain into purpose

Joined by supporters who had witnessed the devastating effects of

addiction on their loved ones, they founded Focus On Recovery -

Uganda (FORE). What began as a small community with a big dream

has since grown into a beacon of hope for those seeking a way out
of the darkness.

FORE is more than just a name; it is a symbol of resilience,healing,

and the power of community. We believe that recovery is not a

destination but a journey—one that is best traveled together. Our

mission is to provide a sanctuary where individuals can find the

strength and support they need to rebuild their lives, free from
the grip of addiction.

Addiction: Problems experienced by the family

The family of a chemically dependent person becomes dependent, counterdependent, codependent and confused. They are victims of the disease of addiction but they may or may not use chemicals, but are nevertheless victimised by the disease.
